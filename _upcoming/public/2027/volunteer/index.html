🐍 Join the Crew: Volunteers wanted for PyCon CZ!

The Python community is built by people like you, and we’re looking for a team of enthusiastic volunteers to help us make this year’s PyCon CZ the best one yet!

Whether you are a seasoned Pythonista or just starting your journey, volunteering is a fantastic way to go behind the scenes, meet the community, and contribute to the vibe of the conference.

How you can help

We have various roles available depending on your interests and skills:

  • Registration & Info Desk: Be the first friendly face attendees see and help them navigate the event.
  • Session Hosting: Introduce speakers and moderate Q&A sessions to keep the talks running smoothly.
  • Venue Support: Help us manage the flow of the crowds and keep the lecture halls organized.
  • Technical Assistance: Provide a hand with AV equipment or general tech troubleshooting.
  • Manual & General Help: Support with the "heavy lifting"—from setting up booths to distributing swag.
  • and more…

Why join us?

Besides our eternal gratitude, you’ll get to experience the conference from a unique perspective, network with speakers and sponsors, and—of course—grab some exclusive volunteer perks.
