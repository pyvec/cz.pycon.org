Board Game Night

Board Game Night starts on Friday right after Lightning talks end {% comment %}in the  Main room{% endcomment %} at 19:10.

No registration is required.

Rules of Board Game Night

  1. You do talk about Board Game Night.
  2. You do talk about Board Game Night.
  3. Anyone can play.
  4. Shirts all time, shoes optional.
  5. Games will go on as long as they have to or until 1 am.
  6. If this is your first night at Board Game Night, you have to play. (Unless you don’t want to.)
  7. You can bring your own board games.
  8. PyCon CZ Code of Conduct applies to Board Game Night.

Also good to know

  • We will bring some games, but you’re welcome to bring your own as well!
  • We can safely store your games during the day, just ask at the registration desk.
  • While English or language-less games are preferred, there will probably be enough Czechs and Slovaks to play Czech games with.
  • Free food for every player!
  • You can buy soft drinks, wine and beer (including non-alcoholic) at the bar at the venue.
  • If board games are not your thing, you are also welcome, just to chat or hack.
  • Musical instruments are also fine, as long as you are not disturbing others (you can always play outside or in another room).
  • Only for PyCon CZ attendees.
