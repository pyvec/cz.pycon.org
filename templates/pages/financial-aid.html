Financial Aid

Financial Aid is available to everyone who would otherwise be unable to attend PyCon CZ because of the costs. This support may cover ticket, accommodation and/or travel costs.

Who can apply for Financial Aid?

Everyone who wants to attend but cannot afford it. We also provide free tickets to those assisting a dissabled attendee.

How challenging is the process?

  • We don’t need proof. You know better than us if you really need Financial Aid. We trust you. We don’t ask about your situation.
  • We only need your contact details and to know what you need: a free ticket or reimbursement of accommodation or travel costs.
  • It’s anonymous – only people who really need your data will have it.
  • It’s easy! Just fill in the form.
  • We’ll let you know the result after 6 August.

Are there any rules?

Only a few, don’t worry.

  • There’s a deadline: You may ask for Financial Aid until the end of July.
  • Financial Aid may only be used for tickets, accommodation and/or travel costs.
  • We will reimburse you based on the invoices, bills or receipts you will provide us.
  • The maximum amount to be granted is 8000 CZK or 350 EUR) per person
  • For more details check Information for Financial Aid Grantees.

Why do we offer Financial Aid?

  • We want everyone to have a chance to attend PyCon CZ!
  • We want to have PyCon CZ as diverse as possible!
  • We believe in the principle of solidarity: those who can afford to pay for tickets should subsidize those who cannot.
